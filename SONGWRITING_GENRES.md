# Genre Questionnaire Worlds (founder-editable)

> Every genre asks its OWN questions. These lists drive the Song Builder: what a song in
> this genre tends to be about (themes), what it's for (purposes), and its instrument
> palette. Deep genres (rooms in SONGWRITING_SUBGENRES.md) narrow these further per room.
> Users can always type their own answer — these are starting points, never walls.
> Format is fixed for the compiler: one `## Genre` heading + three bullets per genre.

## R&B
- **Themes:** New love; Deep love / devotion; Complicated love; Heartbreak / letting go; Missing someone; Celebration / milestone; Family; Faith / gratitude; Growth / proving myself; Remembering someone
- **Purposes:** Slow dance; Bring happy tears; Party / celebrate; Make them feel seen; Testify / give thanks; Win them back; Say what I never said
- **Instrumentation options:** soft keys; 808 sub-bass; live bass; Rhodes piano; saxophone; strings; brushed drums; muted guitar

## Hip-Hop
- **Themes:** The come-up / hustle; Proving them wrong; Where I'm from; Love & loyalty; Losing a friend; Money & ambition; The struggle; Celebration / flexing
- **Purposes:** Flex / celebrate; Tell my story; Motivate the ones grinding; Ride music; Call somebody out; Grieve a loss
- **Instrumentation options:** heavy 808s; boom-bap drums; dusty piano loop; soul sample chops; dark synth lead; strings; live bass

## Gospel
- **Themes:** Praise & worship; Testimony / what God brought me through; Gratitude; Faith through the storm; Deliverance; Celebration of His love; For the congregation
- **Purposes:** Worship / exalt; Testify; Lift the congregation; Comfort in grief; Celebrate; Altar call
- **Instrumentation options:** Hammond organ; grand piano; full choir; tambourine; live bass; gospel drums; handclaps
